About Me

Jonah Manimtim is known for Amateur Hour (2025), Fine Art of Conversation (2025), and Two Die For (2025). He is graduating from Capilano University in Vancouver this year, where he has developed a strong foundation in directing, cinematography, and on‑set collaboration. Jonah has worked extensively on film sets in a wide range of roles, giving him a deep understanding of the full production pipeline. Beyond his professional work, he has created numerous short | films and personal projects that have screened at film festivals, earning him recognition including a short film award. His growing body of work reflects a filmmaker with both technical skill and a clear creative voice
